Mystic's Mission

Our mission is to make unknown things fun & delicious!

We seek to innovate a taste experience that shocks our customers. Due to the shock of lockdown during the 2020 pandemic our founder saw a culture in need of embracing uncertainty & overcoming the fear factor. We offer an exciting spin on dinner dates, kids parties, and work lunches. Step outside of your comfort zone and see how good the unknown can turn out to be!
